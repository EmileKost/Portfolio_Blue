Embark on an exciting cosmic adventure with the NASA Space Quiz project! This interactive web application combines the power of websockets and the NASA API to create a captivating quiz that challenges users to test their knowledge and guess the right planet. Prepare to journey through the vast expanse of the universe as you engage in a series of thought-provoking questions. With the NASA API at its core, the quiz draws upon a wealth of accurate and up-to-date information about our solar system, ensuring an educational and immersive experience for participants of all ages.
